## Why Titles and Descriptions Matter

Titles and descriptions are crucial elements of stock footage metadata. They help buyers find your clips and understand their content at a glance. A well-crafted title paired with a detailed description can significantly influence a buyer’s decision to click on your video.

### How to Write Effective Titles

An effective title should be concise yet descriptive, capturing the essence of your footage. Aim for:

- **Clarity**: Use clear and straightforward language.
- **Keywords**: Include relevant keywords that buyers might use to search for similar footage.
- **Length**: Keep it between 5-10 words for optimal readability.

For example, instead of "Video of a Sunset", try "Dramatic Sunset Over Ocean Waves".

### Crafting Descriptions That Sell

Descriptions should complement your title by providing additional context. Consider including:

- **Detailed Content**: Describe what is happening in the video.
- **Technical Aspects**: Note any significant technical details such as camera movements or lighting.
- **Contextual Information**: Mention the location, time of day, or any relevant background.

A good description might read: "Aerial view of a dramatic sunset casting golden hues over rolling ocean waves, filmed with a steady drone shot."

## Optimizing Stock Footage Metadata

Metadata is the backbone of your video’s discoverability. It includes titles, descriptions, keywords, and other attributes that help categorize and index your footage.

### Importance of Keywords

Keywords bridge the gap between what buyers search for and what you offer. Here’s how to select the right ones:

1. **Relevance**: Ensure keywords accurately represent the content of your video.
2. **Variety**: Use synonyms and related terms to capture a broader audience.
3. **Specificity**: Be specific enough to target a niche but broad enough to reach a wider audience.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

Even experienced contributors can make mistakes when it comes to metadata. Here are a few to watch out for:

- **Overloading with Keywords**: Adding too many keywords can confuse buyers and lower your footage’s search ranking.
- **Vague Titles**: Titles that are too generic fail to attract attention.
- **Lack of Detail in Descriptions**: Skipping on details can leave buyers uncertain about the clip’s content.
