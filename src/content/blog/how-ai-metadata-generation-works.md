## What is AI Metadata Generation?

AI metadata generation involves using artificial intelligence to automatically assign keywords and descriptions to your stock footage. This process leverages machine learning algorithms to analyze the content of your clips and generate metadata that is both relevant and optimized for search engines. By understanding the context and details within each clip, AI can produce accurate metadata that enhances discoverability.

### Why is Metadata Important for Stock Footage?

Metadata is the backbone of your footage's online presence. It includes keywords, titles, and descriptions that help search engines and stock footage platforms understand and categorize your content. Good metadata ensures that your footage appears in relevant search results, making it easier for buyers to find and purchase your clips. Without effective metadata, even the highest quality footage can remain unseen.
