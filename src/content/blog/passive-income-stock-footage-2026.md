## Understanding Stock Footage Metadata and Keywords

Metadata and keywords are crucial for stock footage contributors who want to improve their visibility and sales. Here's how they work:

### What is Stock Footage Metadata?

Metadata is the information attached to your footage that helps buyers find your clips. It includes:

- **Title**: A descriptive name for your clip.
- **Description**: A detailed explanation of what's happening in the footage.
- **Keywords**: Specific terms that potential buyers might use to search for your content.

### How to Choose Effective Keywords

Choosing the right keywords is essential for your footage to appear in search results. Here are some tips:

1. **Be Specific**: Use precise terms that accurately describe your footage.
2. **Think Like a Buyer**: Consider what words a buyer would use to find your clip.
3. **Use a Variety of Keywords**: Include synonyms and related terms to cast a wider net.
